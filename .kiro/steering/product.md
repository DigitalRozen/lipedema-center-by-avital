# Product Overview

## Lipedema Authority Platform

A comprehensive B2C authority platform for lipedema and lymphedema information, combining 80% educational content with 20% monetization engines. The platform serves as a "Lipedema Wiki" with hierarchical content organization and three revenue streams.

### Core Value Proposition
- **Knowledge Hub**: Hierarchical content organization (diagnosis, nutrition, physical therapy, mindset)
- **Smart Product Recommendations**: Contextual affiliate product matching
- **Lead Generation**: High-value clinic consultation leads
- **Content Authority**: Instagram-sourced content establishing expertise

### Target Audience
- Hebrew-speaking women with lipedema/lymphedema
- Seeking reliable medical information and treatment options
- Looking for both self-help solutions and professional care

### Revenue Streams
1. **Contextual Upsell (Affiliate)**: Physical products matched to content
2. **Digital Products (Low Ticket)**: Guides, courses, meal plans
3. **Clinic Leads (High Ticket)**: Personal treatment consultations
